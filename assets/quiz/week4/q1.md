Question
========
1. What is preregistration in the context of research? (Select one)

Answerlist
----------
* Registering for a research conference
* Publishing the final report of a study
* Documenting the plan for a study
* Submitting a research proposal for funding

Solution
========
**Feedback**: Preregistration documents the plan for a study. This is usually before data have been collected, but it can be done after data collection, or when you are using data that already exist (secondary data).

Answerlist
----------
* Registering for a research conference <b>Incorrect</b>
* Publishing the final report of a study <b>Incorrect</b>
* Documenting the plan for a study <b>Correct</b>
* Submitting a research proposal for funding <b>Incorrect</b>
